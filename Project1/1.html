Short history

King Inertia is from Rhode Island. He comes from a musical family and was involved in choir and band growing up, which "eventually got boring to him".

King Inertia first discovered beatboxing in 2015 after watching the final round between Skiller and Alem at the 3rd Beatbox Battle World Championship.

He decided to pursue beatboxing over singing and instruments in an effort to "create something new and be different than everybody else".

He changed his name to King Inertia in 2020.
